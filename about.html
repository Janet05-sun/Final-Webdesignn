About me

Industrial Design student at RISD with a focus on human-centered systems, UI/UX, and emerging interactive technologies. My work sits between physical products and digital experiences, informed by research, prototyping, and experimentation.

I’m especially interested in figuring out AR and VR technologies, how people learn and play within them, and how games and interactive systems can shape behavior, perception, and engagement. I approach design as both a problem-solving and speculative tool, balancing usability with narrative and experience.

My background includes CAD, web design, computational design, and fabrication, along with research methods like ethnography, usability testing, and journey mapping. I enjoy working across disciplines and translating abstract ideas into tangible outcomes.

Currently seeking internships, collaborations, and projects at the intersection of industrial design, UI/UX, and immersive media.
